Our Service

Auditing

At SAF Consulting we assist you to make a smooth and convenient auditing process by following methodical review and objective examination of the financial statements, including the verification of specific information. We keep in mind using the procedures that are suitable for your work and how your business is managed. Throughout the auditing process, we explain the significant points we discovered and the appropriate correcting actions, we also discuss the potential risks that financial statements may contain and the proper way to avoid any extra costs arising from these risks.

Zakat & Tax

Zakat and taxes obligation became very complicated and it has a huge financial effect arising from fines and penalties imposed by regulators as a result of errors in providing information about Zakat and Taxes according to the requirements, instructions, and laws, which are subject to continuous change. At SAF Consulting we assist by making plans and strategies to deal with the rules, regulations, and requirements of Zakat and Taxes, which helps you in reducing the costs and burdens of Zakat and Taxes.

Accounting

Accounting processes might not be your favorite area but it can’t be ignored for its direct effect on your financial position and on your commitments According to the regulations and laws. Managing the accounting processes in the correct way requires lots of time and effort which will affect your focus on your main business. At SAF Consulting we assist you to manage all the accounting process in a way that fits how complex or simple your business is with a high quality and a reasonable cost.

Local content auditing

Local content in Saudi Arabia represents the Kingdom's total expenditure through the inclusion of Saudi elements in the workforce, products, services, manufacturing, and technical resources. This initiative is central to the country's strategy for fostering economic growth and diversification.
