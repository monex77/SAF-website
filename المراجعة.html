١.١ خدمات المراجعة الخارجية

بدلا من طرح منهجية تقليدية بحته لعملية المراجعة، يمكننا تزويدك بتدقيق خارجي مصمم خصيصا ليناسب احتياجاتك واحتياجات عملك ، ويتم إجراؤه بوضوح وتأكيد ومن خلال موارد تقنية وبشرية عالية الكفاءة نستطيع توفير خدمات مراجعة خارجية عالية الجودة.

1.2 Internal Audit And Risk Assessment Services

Our audit team can assist your business in achieving its objectives by reviewing, testing, and evaluating management information systems. We have extensive experience in understanding, documenting, and testing control environments in businesses. This enables us to provide a service that helps your company review, assess, and establish control systems that mitigate the risks surrounding your operations.

1.3 Local Content Reports

We provide a dedicated department with a highly efficient team in local content services. This includes the ability to measure local content at both the company level ("baseline") and at the project level that requires measurement. This applies to planning and related consulting to increase the percentage of local content.

1.4 Forensic Accounting Reports

We have a specialized department for forensic accounting and related expert reports. This includes having a qualified team of technicians with specialized experience in financial matters, liquidation, and accounting expert work for regulatory authorities and appointed accounting expertise.

Audit & Assurance Services

We provide a comprehensive range of consulting services in accounting, specialized services, technology, and risk management for industries, companies, and organizations to assist our clients in achieving their goals and aspirations.

1.5Agreed-Upon Procedures And Related Reports

We provide agreed-upon procedures services according to the scope entrusted to us, in accordance with relevant professional standards applications.

1.6 International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) Services: IFRS, IFRS For SMEs:

Our team has extensive experience in International Accounting Standards and transitioning from various financial frameworks, using the best practices for accounting transition processes for all types of businesses. This is done in alignment with locally or internationally applied financial reporting frameworks and according to the regulations set by auditing and accounting professional bodies.

1.7 Public Sector Accounting Standards (IPSAS) Services:

We have a specialized technical team in government accounting based on accrual basis, following the instructions of IPSAS (International Public Sector Accounting Standards) and best practices directly interacting with regulatory bodies for public sector accounting standards, including locally the Ministry of Finance. Our team also provides comprehensive solutions for transitioning from cash basis to accrual basis and implementing transition or review strategies tailored to achieve the objectives of relevant government or non-profit entities
