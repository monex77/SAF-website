3.1 Accounting Records Management Services

We have a large team of highly qualified accountants and technicians who have undergone extensive training and professional qualifications and have previously worked in various accounting forums and programs. Additionally, our team has the technical capability to select appropriate accounting software and establish a chart of accounts that serves the nature of the company's business. Furthermore, we handle monthly and annual closings and prepare financial statements and important internal reports for decision-making.

3.2 Economic Feasibility Studies Services

We have a team of highly experienced economists who specialize in providing economic consulting services and feasibility studies for business projects in various regional economies. They leverage their previous experiences in various industrial, commercial, and service sectors. Additionally, we utilize our extensive network and incorporate it into our studies, including legal, marketing, supplier and customer lists, and more.

3.3 Financial And Administrative Regulations

Our professional and specialized team carries out the formulation of financial and administrative regulations tailored to the company's activities. This involves studying the current situation of the establishment and proposing an organizational structure suitable for its activities and operations. Additionally, it entails appropriate allocation for each department of the specialized sections, taking into consideration the separation of conflicting functions and providing necessary consultations.

3.4 Company Formation Services

We have a specialized legal team dedicated to establishing commercial enterprises. This involves undertaking all necessary legal procedures for the establishment of business companies, providing appropriate legal advice, and incorporating all accompanying activities for the purpose of setting up business establishments.
