About Us

Welcome to SAF Consulting

Trusted by the best partners in Saudi Arabia

SAF Consulting was established 34 years ago, in 1403 AH, corresponding to 1983 AD, as the first professional company to operate in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia under the license No. 1 registered with the Saudi Organization for Certified Public Accountants (SOCPA). Availingwhatis acquired by its founders through their work with major companies in the world such as Arthur Andersen 1979 – 1980, the company aims to keep pace with the qualitative leap witnessed by the Kingdom in that era and the rapid development in the field of business.

Our Vision
